Creating connection, one design at a time

Hello, I'm CJ Buys [they/them] and I create intuitive, crisp, and beautifully accessible designs.
I believe in designing for "a vulnerable user, instead of an average user", to quote Bumble's Lara Mendonça, and I center this throughout my design process to ensure that every aspect of the design is inherently and seamlessly accessible and welcoming.
